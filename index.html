Fun facts

🥟
Poland invented the pierogi
Poles have been eating these stuffed dumplings since at least the 13th century. Over 700 million pierogi are eaten in Poland every year.
🌲
Home to Europe's last primeval forest
Białowieża Forest spans the border of Poland and Belarus and is the last and largest ancient forest in Europe — home to wild bison.
🎮
A gaming powerhouse
CD Projekt RED, makers of The Witcher and Cyberpunk 2077, is one of the world's most valuable game studios — based in Warsaw.
Did you know?

Poland rebuilt its capital Warsaw almost entirely from scratch after World War II, when 85% of the city was destroyed. The Old Town was reconstructed so faithfully it earned a UNESCO World Heritage Site designation.
